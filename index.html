Expertise

I focus on crafting universal and user centric experiences on the digital products.

Not only do I target my design to be visually aesthetic, but I also try to apply Theory in my design to become products filled with Accessibility, Usability, and so on.

Moreover, I believe that to become a successful product, we need to 3 Factors which are to find understand what user needs, its feasibility, and business vision and mission. My expertise is in finding the sweet spot in which all 3 Factors have a common ground.

  • Product & UXUI Design

    While designing a product, we need not only the aesthetic interface but also a strategic plan to make a user-centric and universal design. My expertise is to create a product that users love, is technically feasible, and can have a profit which means product designing.

  • Enterprise Design Thinking

    As a designer, we cannot work alone. We need to collaborate with other designers, developers, managers, and stakeholders. I have finished IBM's Enterprise Design Thinking Process which helps to create an Enterprise Level Design Thinking Process collaborating with all the stakeholders to create an awesome product.

  • Human Computer Interaction

    Designing a digital product means we are communicating with our users through digital media like mobile and desktop screens. As a self-taught UXUI and Product Designer, I always take care and focus on the technique to communicate with human psychology well through the digital medium.

  • Computer Science

    I have a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science and I would like to craft digital products that people love. So I learned about computer science to craft my products. I can create like MVP for my projects and products with my skillset in Computer Sciences.

  • Entreprenuership

    I have an Entrepreneurship Mindset to find a sweet spot between User Needs, Tech Feasibility, and Business Vision and Mission while creating digital products. And also learning MBA at the moment to use those knowledges in my products.

  • Mentorship

    I founded the Organization called "Design Matters MM" to contribute to my community by teaching them Basic Design Thinking Process and Giving Mentorship Sessions to help solve the problems and blockers they are facing.
